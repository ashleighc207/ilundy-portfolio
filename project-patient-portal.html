myMedstar Microsite

To help advertise the launch of their new Patient Portal and to encourage the creation of new patient accounts, MedStar Health requested a fresh pre-portal microsite. Both the familiar existing and newly tested digital style guides were combined and implemented to create a comfortable, informative and appealing experience for patients who had either never had accounts to start, or who had to update their accounts to the new portal.

created at medstar health

user experience design, icon design

launch site

The development budget for the microsite was low but the impact had to be significant. In addition, what originally started out as a request for a landing page template and one interior page grew as the project progressed. Working closely with the developers three Wordpress templates were created. These templates ranged from the very specific and engaging layout of the landing page to much more modest and flexible templates found in the interior pages. All content within these templates was made easy to update in anticipation of future edits.

Within two months of the microsite going live the site saw over 15,500 engaged users, accounting for 86% of all users. According to the stakeholder, enrollment had well surpassed their goal.
